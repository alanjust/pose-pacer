# Privacy Policy for Pose Pacer

*Last updated: April 23, 2026*

Pose Pacer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device. No account is required. No internet connection is used. No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind is included.

All session data (presets, settings) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
